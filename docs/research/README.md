# UniSchedule AI — Primary Research Pack (Week 5)

This folder contains instruments and checklists for Week 4–5 research catch-up. **Do not distribute** survey or interview materials until ethics approval is confirmed with your supervisor and university process.

## Launch checklist

| Step | Document | Status |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Confirm ethics pathway with supervisor | ☐ |
| 2 | Submit ethics application (if required) | ☐ |
| 3 | Finalise information sheet + consent | ☐ |
| 4 | Publish survey (target n ≥ 20) | ☐ |
| 5 | Schedule 3–5 interviews | ☐ |
| 6 | Log responses in analysis spreadsheet | ☐ |
